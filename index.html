Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How far in advance should I book my trip?

+

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance for international trips and 1 month for domestic travel. This ensures better availability, pricing, and allows us to create the perfect itinerary for you. However, we also handle last-minute bookings based on availability.

What is included in the package price?

+

Package prices typically include accommodation, meals (as specified), airport transfers, guided tours, and travel insurance. Flight costs may vary and can be included upon request. Each package clearly lists what's included, and we can customize based on your preferences.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Free cancellation up to 7 days before departure with full refund. Cancellations within 7 days are subject to a 50% fee. No refunds within 48 hours of departure. Full refunds are provided if flights or hotels become unavailable due to circumstances beyond your control.

Do you offer travel insurance?

+

Yes, comprehensive travel insurance is included in all our packages. It covers medical emergencies, trip cancellations, lost baggage, flight delays, and more. We highly recommend keeping the insurance active for international trips. Additional coverage can be added upon request.

Can I customize my travel package?

+

Absolutely! All our packages are fully customizable. You can modify destinations, accommodation types, activities, meal plans, and trip duration. Our travel experts will work with you to create a personalized itinerary that matches your preferences and budget perfectly.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, bank transfers, and UPI payments. For bookings over $2000, we offer flexible payment plans with 0% interest. A deposit of 30% is required to confirm your booking.

Do I need a visa for international travel?

+

Visa requirements vary by destination and your nationality. Our team will provide complete guidance on visa requirements and assist with the application process. We recommend checking visa requirements at least 3 months before travel to ensure timely processing.

What if I need to make changes to my booking?

+

Most bookings can be modified up to 7 days before departure. Changes to dates, accommodations, or activities are subject to availability and may incur additional fees. Contact our support team as soon as possible to discuss your requirements, and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs.
