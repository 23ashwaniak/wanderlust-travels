Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

+

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance for international trips and 1 month for domestic travel. This ensures better availability and pricing.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Free cancellation up to 7 days before departure with full refund. Cancellations within 7 days are subject to a 50% fee. No refunds within 48 hours of departure.

Do you offer travel insurance?

+

Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance covering medical emergencies, trip cancellations, lost baggage, and more. We highly recommend it for all international trips.

Can I customize my package?

+

Absolutely! All our packages can be customized to suit your preferences. Contact us with your requirements and we'll create a personalized itinerary.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, bank transfers, and offer flexible payment plans for bookings over $2000.
